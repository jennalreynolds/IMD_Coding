About Us

Holes was founded in 2025 by Jenna Reynolds. Her first job working in a donut shop for five years where she came up with many donut creations when she was bored sparked an idea. With a love for mini golf and sweets, Holes was born. Holes is meant to be a place for people of all ages to come and have fun and let off some steam from the pressures of real life. Holes offers the fun interactive elements of mini golf with the treat built it.
